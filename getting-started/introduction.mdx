## Discover Pylon

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card
    title="Conversational Ticketing"
    icon="comments"
    color="#ea5a0c"
    href="/ticketing"
  >
    Track all conversations across prospect and customer channels. Easily triage
    messages, assign conversations, get team visibility, track SLAs, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Support" icon="robot" color="#880808" href="/support/ai">
    Leverage AI to auto-respond to questions already answered in your
    documentation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Broadcasts" icon="paper-plane" href="/broadcast">
    Send a message to many channels at once and get live engagement data
    including link clicks, reactions, and replies. Use them for product updates,
    outages, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="In-App Chat"
    icon="message-captions"
    color="#d4cd16"
    href="/chat"
  >
    Embed an In-App Chat into your application so your customers can get support
    anywhere. Messages sync back to your shared customer channel so the entire
    team gets context on the issue.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Support Ticketing" icon="ticket" color="#16a34a" href="/support">
    Handoff support questions to the support team while letting the customer
    continue asking questions in Slack. All support related questions will be
    logged in your support system.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CRM Data Sync" icon="users" color="#dc2626" href="/crm">
    Auto-summarize and log conversations, and create new contacts in your CRM
    for new customers that join enabled channels. Use CRM data within Pylon to
    filter by attributes.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Analytics"
    icon="chart-simple"
    color="#FFC0CB"
    href="/ticketing/analytics"
  >
    Get data on number of Customer Channels, Tickets, Conversations, TTFR, TTR,
    Issues over Time, Last Activity, and more, all filterable by CRM data.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Chat Bridges"
    icon="microsoft"
    color="#0285c7"
    href="/bridges/teams"
  >
    Stay in Slack while your chatting with your customers who use Microsoft
    Teams, Discord, WhatsApp.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Product Ticketing"
    icon="ticket"
    color="#e5e500"

    Asana, and Linear.
