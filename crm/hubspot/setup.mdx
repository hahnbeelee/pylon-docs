<Note>You'll need a Salesforce administrator to complete the setup.</Note>

<Tip>
  If you already have the Salesforce Service Integration installed, you can
  simply hit `Connect` under Salesforce on the [Integrations
  Page](https://app.usepylon.com/integrations).
</Tip>

1. Send the Pylon team your Salesforce subdomain in our shared Slack channel.
   You can find your subdomain in your Salesforce URL
   `https://{subdomain}.salesforce.com`.
2. Visit the [Integrations Page](https://app.usepylon.com/integrations) and hit
   `Connect` under Salesforce

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/sfdc-install.png" />
</Frame>
3. Click `Authorize` on the Salesforce Authorization Page

4. Click `Manage` on the Salesforce Integration and Confirm the User Mapping is
   set correctly. The User Mapping allows us map Salesforce Users to Slack Users

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/sfdc-user-mapping.png" />
</Frame>

All done!

<Check>You've setup our Salesforce Integration</Check>
