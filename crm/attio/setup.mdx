<Note>You'll need a Attio administrator to complete the setup.</Note>

1. Visit the [Integrations Page](https://app.usepylon.com/integrations) and hit
   `Connect` under Attio

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/attio-install.png" />
</Frame>

2. Click `Authorize` on the Attio Authorization Page
3. Click `Manage` on the Attio Integration and confirm the User Mapping is set
   correctly. The User Mapping allows us to map Attio Users to Slack Users

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/attio-user-mapping.png" />
</Frame>

All done!

<Check>You've setup our Attio Integration</Check>
