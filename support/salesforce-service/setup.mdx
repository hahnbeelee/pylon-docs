<Note>You'll need a Salesforce administrator to complete the setup.</Note>

1. Visit the [Integrations Page](https://app.usepylon.com/integrations) and hit
   `Connect` under Salesforce Service
2. Authorize the Salesforce Service App
3. Click `Manage` on the Salesforce Service App and confirm the user mapping is
   set properly

All done!

<Check>You've setup the Salesforce Service Integration</Check>
