<Note>You'll need admin permissions in Google Workspace.</Note>

The only setup needed is automatic forwarding from your support email to us.

1. Visit the [Workflows Page](https://app.usepylon.com/workflows) and go to the Email tab.
1. Set your support email in the Receiving Email field.
1. Copy the email on the config page and follow the [google link instructions](https://support.google.com/a/answer/4524505#address-map-forwarding) to setup forwarding.
1. Click `Test` to verify that email forwarding is setup properly.

All done!

<Check>You've setup the Email Integration</Check>
